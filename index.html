About the Show

Welcome to Nookieland

Nookieland is a live-action preschool program from Midnight Entertainment that celebrates curiosity, problem-solving, and friendship. In a vibrant, tactile world, five raccoon-like friends called Nookies discover that the biggest adventures can happen in their own backyard.

The Magic of an Idea

Whenever one of the Nookies has a bright idea, the special shape on their belly lights up! This visual cue helps preschoolers connect the "Aha!" moment of discovery with the fun of finding a solution. It's a key part of how the show encourages creative thinking.

Behind the Fur

The Nookies are brought to life through a blend of physical performance and advanced animatronics. Talented actors wear detailed, full-body costumes, while puppeteers off-screen control the intricate movements of each Nookie's face. This allows for a range of emotions and expressions that make the characters feel truly alive.

Parents' Corner

Our Educational Goals

Hello Parents! At Midnight Entertainment, we designed Nookieland to be more than just a fun show. Our curriculum is focused on fostering key developmental skills for preschoolers:

  • Cognitive Development: Encouraging critical thinking and problem-solving through the Nookies' challenges.
  • Social-Emotional Learning: Modeling positive friendship, empathy, and collaboration.
  • Creative Expression: Inspiring imagination and celebrating unique ideas.
  • Foundational STEAM: Introducing basic concepts of shapes, colors, patterns, and simple physics in a playful context.

We believe that learning should be a joyful adventure, and we're thrilled to have your family join us in Nookieland!
